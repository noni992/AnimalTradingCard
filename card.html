Jellyfish

Sea jelly is a member of the subphylum Medusozoa, a major part of the phylum Cnidaria. Jellyfish are eaten by humans in certain cultures.You need to be careful while dealing with jellyfish;some of it are toxic.

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • phylum: Cnidaria
  • Subphylum:: Medusozoa
  • Where to find: all over the world, from surface waters to the deep sea.

Jellyfish are mainly free-swimming marine animals with umbrella-shaped bells and trailing tentacles. There are many type of jellyfish as:Acraspeda,Cubozoa—box jellyfish,Scyphozoa—true jellyfish,Staurozoa—stalked jellyfish,some Hydrozoa—small jellyfish.
